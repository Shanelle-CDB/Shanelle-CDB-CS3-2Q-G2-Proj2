Are you curious about Cyberbullying?

If you are, then this page is for you. Cyberbullying, in simple terms, is bullying online. It usually takes place on messaging platforms, gaming platforms, or social media to threaten, harass, or embarrass another person. This issue is a serious problem worldwide. It affects people of all ages and can have serious effects on mental health, self-esteem, and even physical well-being.
